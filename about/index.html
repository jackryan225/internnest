Our mission

Internships should not come down to who you know

InternNest exists to give every student the kind of personalized, well-connected guidance that used to be reserved for a lucky few.

Finding an internship is one of the most stressful parts of college — and the playing field is far from level. Students with the right network get warm intros and insider advice. Everyone else is left refreshing job boards full of dead links and guessing what recruiters actually want.

We built InternNest to close that gap. Tell us about yourself, and our AI ranks real, verified internships against your profile — explaining why each one fits, what skills to build, and how to reach out. It is the advisor, the network, and the application coach, in one place.

What we believe

  • Real or nothing. Every internship we surface links to a live application page. We would rather show you fewer real roles than pad the list with noise.
  • Show the why. A match score means nothing without a reason. We always explain our thinking so you stay in control.
  • Built for students. Student-friendly pricing, no subscriptions, no fluff — just the help you actually need to land the offer.

Where we are headed

InternNest is early and growing fast. We are continuously expanding our verified internship database and sharpening the AI behind every match. This is just the beginning.
