How and why InternNest uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: June 30, 2026. This is a starting template and should be reviewed by a qualified professional before launch. Bracketed items must be completed by InternNest.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites function and understand usage.

2. How we use them

We use essential cookies and local storage to run core features — for example, remembering a Premium unlock or your application tracker on your device. We may use limited analytics to improve the service.

3. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings. Note that clearing storage will reset a device-based Premium unlock or saved tracker items.
